Editorial boundary

What every issue must disclose.

  • Observation: source date, collection window, and measured change.
  • Assessment: the editor's current interpretation and known gaps.
  • Forecast: horizon, uncertainty, assumptions, method, evidence, and limitations.
  • Freshness: the latest accepted product run, including any deferred publication.
