Guardrails

No black-box intelligence surface.

Agents may retrieve references, templates, and published data files. They should not invent scores, fabricate country judgments, or imply unpublished methodology.

  • Country pages are baseline references.
  • Index outputs require source-backed methodology.
  • Published JSON is authoritative for static data access.
  • Human-edited analysis remains separate from machine-readable records.
